## 💬 General Conversation

Anything that doesn't match a specific command above will be sent to the AI assistant (Lumo) for a natural conversation response.

**Examples:**
- "Tell me a joke"
- "What's the meaning of life?"
- "Give me a motivational quote"
- "How far is the moon?"

---

## 🎯 Tips for Best Results

1. **Speak clearly** — Face the mirror and speak at a normal volume
2. **Wait for the cyan glow** — The orb turns cyan when Lumo is listening for your command
3. **Keep it short** — Commands work best when concise (e.g., "pause music" instead of "can you please pause the music for me")
4. **One command at a time** — Say one command, wait for the response, then give the next
5. **Background noise** — Minimize background noise for better recognition
6. **Automatic muting** — The mic auto-mutes during TTS playback to prevent feedback loops
